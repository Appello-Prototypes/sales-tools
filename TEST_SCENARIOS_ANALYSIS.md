## System Capability Analysis

### Scoring System Performance

**Expected Score Distribution:**
- **High Priority (75+):** Scenarios 1, 3, 4, 6, 7, 9 (6 scenarios)
- **Medium Priority (55-74):** Scenarios 2, 10 (2 scenarios)
- **Low Priority (<55):** Scenarios 5, 8 (2 scenarios)

**Scoring Factors Validated:**
1. ✅ **Urgency** - Scenarios with 9-10 urgency score higher
2. ✅ **Company Size** - Larger companies (100+) score higher
3. ✅ **Union Complexity** - Multiple CBAs increase score
4. ✅ **Pain Point Count** - More pain points = higher score
5. ✅ **Timeline** - Shorter timelines increase score
6. ✅ **Current State** - Manual systems score higher than digital

### Feature Analysis Performance

**Module Recommendations Validated:**
- **Workforce Admin:** Union scenarios (1, 3, 4, 7, 9)
- **Work Orders:** Service work scenarios (3, 10)
- **Material Management:** Material pain scenarios (1, 5, 6, 7, 9)
- **Financials:** Job costing/billing scenarios (1, 2, 4, 5, 6, 7)
- **Safety & Compliance:** Government/compliance scenarios (4, 9)
- **AI-Powered Intelligence:** Enterprise scenarios (7)
- **Sales & Estimation:** Estimating gap scenarios (5)

**Key Differentiators Identified:**
- ✅ Union Payroll Mastery (scenarios 1, 4, 7, 9)
- ✅ Service Work Management (scenarios 3, 10)
- ✅ Material Management (scenarios 1, 6, 7, 9)
- ✅ Government Contract Ready (scenario 4)
- ✅ AI-Powered Intelligence (scenario 7)
- ✅ Integrated Platform (scenario 7)

### ROI Calculation Performance

**ROI Factors Validated:**
- ✅ **Time Costs:** Higher for companies with more hours/week
- ✅ **Profit Margin Loss:** Calculated based on pain points
- ✅ **Company Size:** Larger companies = higher absolute savings
- ✅ **Union Complexity:** Additional costs for complex payroll
- ✅ **Payback Period:** Shorter for high-urgency scenarios

**Expected ROI Ranges:**
- **Large Companies (100+):** $100K-$500K+ annual savings
- **Mid-Size (20-99):** $50K-$200K annual savings
- **Small (1-19):** $20K-$80K annual savings
